| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|

Bounds the prompt-injection + context-bloat surface at the source |
| T-17-08 | Denial of Service | furnace session hang / leak | mitigate | Single-flight FURNACE_SESSION with OPEN/TRANSFER timeouts via Promise.race (container template) + closeFurnaceSession wired into closeAnySessions chain-end cleanup (Task 1 acceptance) |
